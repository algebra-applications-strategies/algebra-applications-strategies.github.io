Principle 2.2.1. Connection between an equation in \(x\) and \(y\) and its graph.

Every equation in \(x\) and \(y\) has a graph, which is the set of all points \((x,y)\) that satisfy the equation. The point \((x,y)\) is on the graph of the equation if and only if it satisfies the equation.
