Example 1.6.66.

Factor \(x^2 + 6x + 9\text{.}\)
🔗
Answer 1.
To apply Principle 1.6.65, we identify \(a=x\) and \(b=3\text{.}\) Then,
\begin{equation*} x^2 + 6x + 9 = (x+3)^2. \end{equation*}
