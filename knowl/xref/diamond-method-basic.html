Strategy 1.6.40.

To factor \(x^2+Bx+C\text{,}\) draw the following diagram: The remaining two spaces (on the left and right) need to be filled with two numbers that multiply to the number \(C\) at the top of the diamond, and add to \(B\) at the bottom of the diamond. It is often helpful to write all the factor pairs of \(C\text{,}\) and then go through the list to see which pair of numbers adds to \(B\text{.}\)
