Example 2.1.15.

Find the distance between \((z,y_1)\) and \((z,y_2)\text{,}\) under the assumption \(y_2>y_1\text{.}\)
🔗
Answer.
Since the two points have the same \(x\)-coordinate (which was labeled with the letter \(z\) to represent a mystery value), the distance is \(y_2-y_1\text{.}\)
