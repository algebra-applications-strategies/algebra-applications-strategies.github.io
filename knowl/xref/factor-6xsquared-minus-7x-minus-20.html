The pair that adds to \(-7\) is \(8\) and \(-15\text{.}\) We factor
\begin{equation*} 6x^2 + 8x - 15x - 20. \end{equation*}
Applying Factoring by Grouping, \(6x^2 + 8x - 15x - 20 = (6x^2 + 8x) + (-15x - 20) = 2x(3x + 4) - 5(3x + 4) = (2x - 5)(3x + 4).\)
